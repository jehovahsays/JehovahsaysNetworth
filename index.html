  • Privacy

    The MEV project is built on the principle of Hardware-Software Sovereignty. Unlike traditional web applications, your data does not belong to a cloud; it belongs to your machine.

    1. Local-First Storage

    We do not use tracking cookies. We utilize Web Storage (localStorage) to maintain your session state, humanity verification, and security flags. This data never leaves your browser and is never transmitted to a central server.

    2. Zero Third-Party Dependencies

    This application is built from scratch. No external trackers, analytics, or third-party scripts are permitted to touch your internal "Subconscious" brain. The only external entity allowed is the maintainer's whitelist for software updates.

    3. Your Right to Delete

    To wipe all trace of this application, simply clear your browser's "Site Data." Since we hold no copies of your data on a server, your local deletion is final and absolute.
