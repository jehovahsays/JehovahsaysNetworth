# 🧭 Code of Conduct — MEV Project

We value respectful, inclusive participation.

---

## 📌 Our Standards

- Be kind and respectful
- No harassment or abuse
- Help newcomers
- Keep issues and comments constructive

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- Offensive comments
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Threats or harassment

---

## 🙋 Reporting Issues

To report abusive behavior:

- Open a confidential GitHub issue
- Or contact a maintainer privately

---

## 🧾 Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent removal from the community

---

## ❤️ Thank You

We appreciate every contributor and user.
